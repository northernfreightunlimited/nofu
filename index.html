Rules

Please read the following rules before creating a contract.

Northern Freight Unlimited

Create your contract to Northern Freight Unlimited. Ticker is [NOFU].

7/7 day Accept/Complete

Set your contract for 7 days to accept and 7 days to complete. Most contracts are completed within 48 hours but this is not a guarantee.

Rush or Custom Quotes

Join our discord and contact Ackbarre, Ajaxify, or Parrth.

$30m ISK Minimum

Unless otherwise sepecified, a 30m ISK minimum reward applies to all contracts.

Maximum 10b ISK Collateral

Max 10b ISK collateral per contract.

Maximum 335k m3

Max 335k m3 per contract.

Blue Characters

All contracts leaving 1DQ1 must be from blue characters.

No Containers

No containers. If you need containers moved for some reason please reach out directly.

No Black Frog banned systems

No contracts to or from systems banned by Black Frog. Exceptions for Imperium systems.

Questions?

Please join our Discord Server.
